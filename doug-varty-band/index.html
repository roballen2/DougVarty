Classic Blues Rock

Doug Varty Band

A very enjoyable blues rock band that knows when to rock out and when to lay back. Canadian Doug Varty has been around the block a few times, playing with such respected figures as Jeff Healey, Paul Rodgers and Bob Seger. He and his band are proponents of blues rock in a very traditional way. Whether filling the room with screaming guitar Rock Clasics or a suttle ballad. They’re equally competent at everything they do.
Doug Varty

Canadian blues rock artist and tribute performer, London, Ontario.
