Beatles in Blue

Spinning the Beatles songbook, a new way, this talented trio serves up Fab Four material a la Texas Boogie, Chicago Shuffle, New Orleans Funk and a whole lot more. Beatles in Blue brings you a blues reworking of Beatle classics that showcases slide guitar, blues harp, piano, soulful vocals, and a powerhouse rhythm section. Imagine “Birthday” done by ZZ Top, “Can’t Buy Me Love” by Bo Diddley, and “Ticket To Ride” by The Black Keys. You’re getting the idea.

The Band Members are:

Lead singer and guitar Doug Varty

Bass and Vocals Paul Loeffelholz

Drums and Vocals Corey Thompson

Guitarist extraordinaire Iain McNally

Together and separately, they’ve won awards and received accolades for their accomplishments. Beatles in Blue’s mastery and energy comes through in every joyful performance.
