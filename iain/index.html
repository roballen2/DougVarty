Beatles in Blue

Iain

IAIN

McNALLY

Versatile musician/producer/songwriter, Iain McNally plays guitar, piano, bass, and percussion. He’s toured around the world backing recording artists, appreared on NBC Late Night, jammed with big stars and played some of the biggest venues in the world.

He currently owns and operates I-Level Studio where he does most all of his recording work these days. His production and songwriting have won many awards and his guitar playing can be heard on top 10 hits.

Originally studying at Humber Music College, Iain was recently awarded Forest City London Music Awards - Fantasy Band Choice - for best guitarist in 2022 and 2023. He’s also well known for his solo jazz guitar performances throughout Ontario.

As Beatles in Blue’s newest member, Iain has brought the band new musical depth with his emotional soloing and inventive rhythmic and sonic ideas. His soulful playing will often bring cheering audiences to their feet.
