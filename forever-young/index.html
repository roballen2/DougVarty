The Ultimate Tribute to Rod Stewart

Forever Young

Doug Varty pays a music tribute to “Rod” Stewart British rock singer-songwriter. DOUG VARTY’S ‘Having a Party’ The Ultimate Tribute to ROD STEWART. It takes a special kind of performer to create the voice and stage presence of Rod Stewart, the timeless vocalist known for hits like “Hot Legs,” “Forever Young”, “Maggie May” and “Do Ya Think I’m Sexy.” Doug Varty is that performer. And just like Rod, whenever he performs, Doug Varty makes the stage (and the surrounding area) his playground, interacting with the crowd, dancing with, and singing to audience members. “It’s about creating something special, something memorable.

Doug Varty

Canadian blues rock artist and tribute performer, London, Ontario.
